About Me

My name is Adam Rizzo and I am a self-taught freelance programmer. I have been actively learning programming - namely JavaScript and C# over the past five years. Most of my free time is spent trying to extend my knowledge in the fields of web and software development.

My current career is as an 8th grade math teacher. This is my 10th year in the profession and I currently hold a Master's degree in math education. Beore teaching math, I was an instructional technology teacher at the same middle school. To me, teaching and learning go hand-in-hand and you can't be great at one of them if you stop doing the other. When it comes to programming, I took it upon myself to try to teach others in need when I could. These two articles - HERE and HERE were my attempts to give back to those who needed help.

While I love numbers and technology, my true passion is problem solving. Whether that's with a difficult math problem, debugging code or learning a new programming language, I thrive on being challenged mentally.

Whether you are looking for website/web app or developing a new piece of software for your business to utilize on a day-to-day basis, I want to help you reach your potential however I can! If you decide that I'm someone you'd love to work with, you're getting an individual that excels with communication, customization and a tireless work ethic.
